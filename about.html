About StarChart

Open-source cosmic blueprint calculator with astronomical precision

What is StarChart?

StarChart is a free, open-source tool that calculates your cosmic blueprint using three complementary wisdom systems. By entering your birth date, time, and location, you receive a comprehensive profile that synthesizes ancient wisdom with modern astronomical precision.

Unlike many online calculators that use simplified approximations, StarChart employs the same astronomical algorithms used in professional planetarium software, ensuring accuracy within one arcminute (1/60th of a degree).

Western Astrology

The oldest of the three systems, Western astrology maps the positions of celestial bodies at the moment of your birth. StarChart calculates your complete natal chart including:

  • Sun, Moon, and Rising (Ascendant) signs - your "Big Three"
  • All planetary positions: Mercury through Pluto
  • Lunar Nodes (North and South)
  • Midheaven (MC) for career and public life
  • Element and modality balance

Human Design

Synthesized in 1987 by Ra Uru Hu, Human Design combines astrology, the I Ching, Kabbalah, and the Hindu Chakra system. It reveals your energetic blueprint:

  • Type - Your fundamental energy pattern (Generator, Projector, Manifestor, Reflector, or Manifesting Generator)
  • Strategy - How you're designed to make decisions
  • Authority - Your inner guidance system
  • Profile - Your costume in life's play
  • Centers, Gates, and Channels - Your unique circuitry
  • Incarnation Cross - Your life theme

Gene Keys

Created by Richard Rudd as an evolution of Human Design, Gene Keys focuses on the transformational journey from shadow to gift to siddhi. StarChart calculates your complete Golden Path:

  • Activation Sequence - Life's Work, Evolution, Radiance, Purpose
  • Venus Sequence - Attraction, IQ, EQ, SQ (relationships)
  • Pearl Sequence - Vocation, Culture, Pearl (prosperity)

Open Source Philosophy

StarChart is completely open source under the MIT license. This means:

  • Free to use for any purpose
  • Full source code available on GitHub
  • No tracking, analytics, or data collection
  • All calculations happen in your browser - no data leaves your device
  • You can fork, modify, and build upon this project

We believe cosmic wisdom should be accessible to everyone, without paywalls or privacy concerns.
